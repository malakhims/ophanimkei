100 Lolita Questions

1. Please tell us your screen name, date of birth, and origin.
I'm Malaika though I go by Mala. I was born July 25th, and I'm from the United States.

2. What is your height, weight, clothing size, and shoe size?
I'm 5'4. Guess the rest!

3. What made you start doing Lolita fashion?
I've been interested in the fashion for several years and finally got enough money and space from my family to order a dress! The final push was my friend Io teaching me how to use Taobao.

4. What is your favorite Lolita genre?
I'm a big goth and classic. Glassic? I also love old school in various substyles but especially punk and casual.

5. Do you have any particular commitment to that genre, or your own definition of it?
I usually look for pieces that can go with nu old school or old school itself though some pieces I buy are inspired by old school but a bit too modern to even be considered nu. I guess it is my own spin on it, but really, I just buy what I like and my tastes tend to be consistent.

6. Do you have your ears pierced? How many piercings?
Yes, but I'm not sure they count. My ears are pierced. Just one piercing on both sides. I've had them pierced since I was a baby

7. What is your favorite brand?
I honestly don't know... Perhaps H Naoto or N+ah but I don't own many pieces from them (yet)

8. What is your favorite outfit?
I love going blouseless in BTSSB's babydoll jsk. A cardigan addition is also cute. Sock can very. It's a comfy outfit I feel consistently cute it.

9. Lolita How many friends do you have?
So many! So many! I can't count them!

10. What was the best thing about doing Lolita?
I love the connections I've made in the community- online and offline. It's gotten me out of the house, and I've spoken to people I never imagined speaking to! I also love speaking to Chinese lolitas.

11. On the contrary, what is the most disgusting thing?
I dislike the lack of research common among people in the subculture. It makes me uncomfortable.

12. What is your favorite magazine?
I like Fruits and Kera, but I don't actively collect them.

13. Headdress group? Team Bonnet? Team Headbow?
I like old school rectangular headpieces though lately I've been getting into berets. I also love headbows but unfortunately they can give me headaches.

14. Do you have bloomers?
Yes, I love bloomers. They help me feel safe.

15. Do you tie your headdress in front? or are you on the team that ties it behind your head?
I tied them in the back typically. I find the sensory experience of tying them in the front to be a bit unbearable.

16. Do you have a favorite music genre or band?
My favorite band is Panchiko, and lately I've been listening to a variety of gothic genres.

17. What is your mobile phone ringtone?
It's just the default apple ringtone. I mainly call my girlfriend and family. I thought about changing it to a song, but I got embarrassed thinking about it playing in innapropriate settings.

I saw Machine Girl in lolita!

20. What color do you like?
Black, pink, red, white

Art, coding, writing, game deving, gaming, etc

23. Is there anything you say "I'm careful about this" when it comes to lolita?
I'm careful with laundry, and I try not to get lace stuck on things.

24. Where are the places you visit / hang out at frequently?
I visit the cemetery often. It is a go to spot. I realize it is inherently strange and can easily get me judged, but I really just find it to be a nice place. There's usually not a lot of people (and if there is a group of people, you can just go to the other side to not disturb them). I also like to talk to the graves and connect various family members. I'm fascinated by last names that reveals a family's ethnic background. I think people when they're buried can get lonely. If ghosts are real, that means they're connected to their physical bodies or place of death!

25. How many times a week do you wear Lolita?
I wear lolita whenever I don't have to go to work!

To be honest every time I start to crash out in lolita I feel embarrassed.

I've been doing twist outs for the last few days. It's black.

61. Just between us, is there a store that makes you say: 'that's a rip-off!'?
Basic answer but Moi Meme Moitie. I really do try to see the brightside of their modern designs, butI always end up thinking the fabric and cuts are weird. The photos on their social media are actually getting better lately, yet the clothes themselves still look terrible.
And well, I also think Closetchild over prices things. I don't really like that store much anymore unless I'm buying something really casual.

62. Have you ever used an online shop? If you have, please give us some advice on the good points and bad points. If you haven't, tell us why not.
Yes though I've shopped in person at Nightshade and in Japan too. I think Chinese resellers are often too overpriced- even the ones I think fondly of, but I simply think DevilInspired is the worst of them all. Their are so many beautiful Chinese dresses, yet Devilinspired will stock something that has horrible reviews or is made of terrible polyester.
Also,you need to know your measurements. You can't just guess because each brand operates differently and the sizing can vary each release. Even being a few cm off can ruin an item for you.

75. Other than what you said 73, what is something you wish people wouldn't do?
I wish people recognized there are a lot of beautiful Chinese brand items despite the questionable ones we're often exposed to... People make broad generalizations about them because of DI garbage and well, even some stuff 42lolita sells isn't fantastic, but well, I feel like with all things in this fashion, if you spend time to really look through the market, you'll see interesting Chinese brands are.
Also I dislike when people use ita too much. Just say you don't like something. People can be so boring and expect everyone else to be boring like them. There's things that certainly are ita but a coord with a few elements you personally dislike isn't ita.
