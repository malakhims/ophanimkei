flights of fancy

When I was a kid, I used to watch a lot of vloggers from various subcultures (emo, goth, lolita, etc). I always really wanted to be like them and would record myself talking to my webcam. I never uploaded any of it of course, but I just considered it practice I guess?

I noticed a lot of old schoolers enjoy making video content by way of challenges or travel vlogs or wardrobe tours, so I wanted to pick up the hobby too. It takes me a long time to edit content as video editing is not something I enjoy, so uploads will be sparse, but.. I hope my younger me is somewhere smiling knowing I'm finally uploading things.
