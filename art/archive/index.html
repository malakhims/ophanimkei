Hello! Welcome to my little museum, my archive. I've been making art for a long time now. I'm not sure how long. I began to write at a young age, and at some point, I wanted to start seeing my characters on paper. I began to read comics too, and got interested in that medium. These archive goes back to 2017, but I know I started drawing seriously a while before that. It's just difficult to pin the exact date as those years are a blur now. I hope you enjoy looking through my archive!
